What we do

We broker between licensed fleets and mobility and logistics platforms. When platforms need capacity, we find it. When fleets need volume, we bring it.

01

Fleet brokerage

We connect mobility and logistics platforms with licensed fleets across DACH. Vetting, contracts, coordination, all on our side.

  • Single accountable counterparty for platform partners
  • Commercial negotiation with mobility and logistics platforms
  • Multi-platform allocation strategy across fleet capacity
  • Performance accountability and operational reporting

02

Operational advisory

We help mobility and logistics platforms enter markets and build supply. Direct experience from Wolt, Bolt, and Bliq.

  • Fleet growth strategy and partner acquisition
  • Driver retention systems and onboarding design
  • KPI systems and operational dashboards
  • Market expansion and product localization

03

Technical capacity

  • API integrations and data infrastructure
  • DevOps and cloud architecture (through technical partners)
  • Custom operational dashboards and reporting tools
