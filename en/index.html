About

343rides is a Berlin-based mobility brokerage operated by Salas UG (haftungsbeschränkt). The company combines senior operational experience across European mobility marketplaces with strategic fleet partnership capacity in Berlin, serving the platform-and-fleet ecosystem across DACH.

343rides operates in strategic partnership with an established Berlin-based fleet operator providing regulated capacity (Mietwagen Konzession, vehicles, and licensed drivers). This structure combines operational rigor with commercial sophistication — platforms get a sophisticated counterparty; fleets get senior representation in platform negotiations.
