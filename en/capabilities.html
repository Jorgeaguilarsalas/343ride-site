Capabilities · Berlin · DACH

Operational backbone

Four areas we bring from building and operating European mobility and logistics platforms. Not theory, but measurable experience in driver acquisition, compliance infrastructure, commercial scaling, and operational analytics.

Fleet operations

Building, scaling, and steering the supply side. From the first driver contract to retention strategy across multiple cities at once.

  • Driver acquisition and onboarding funnels
  • Activation and retention programs
  • Performance management at city and fleet level
  • Incentive design and payout structures
  • Supply-demand balancing
  • Escalation workflows and driver support structures
Concrete experience Increased driver retention at a European mobility marketplace from below 50% to over 80% within twelve months. Owned courier operations for an on-demand delivery platform in a major European market.

Commercial and go-to-market

Market entry, partner sourcing, and commercial scaling. From direct experience building marketplaces for Wolt, Bolt, and Bliq, as well as logistics operations at Amazon and Hermes.

  • Market analysis and city prioritization for market entry
  • Partner sourcing and account management on the fleet side
  • Platform onboarding and commercial contracts
  • Pricing and margin structuring
  • KPI frameworks for GTM teams
  • Operational handover between sales and operations
Concrete experience Supply build-up across multiple European cities. Operational interface between commercial teams and fleet partners in DACH and Northern Europe.

Compliance and infrastructure

Regulatory requirements for passenger transport and logistics in Germany and the DACH region. With direct experience in Mietwagen-Konzession management and authority reporting.

  • Mietwagen-Konzession tracking and renewal workflows
  • LABO reporting and documentation compliance
  • Driver document monitoring (Führerschein, P-Schein, Personenbeförderungsschein)
  • Audit preparation for authorities and platform partners
  • Vehicle compliance and TÜV tracking
  • Legally sound contract structures between platforms and fleets
Concrete experience Built a compliance framework for a German fleet management platform, including automated document monitoring across multiple fleets.

Technology and analytics

Operational dashboards, reporting structures, and data analysis for mobility and logistics operations. Data as a steering instrument, not decoration.

  • Driver funnel analytics and conversion tracking
  • Operational dashboards (Looker, Tableau, Streamlit, custom)
  • SQL-based reporting and data exploration
  • Cohort retention and lifetime-value modeling
  • Performance monitoring at city, fleet, and driver level
  • Automated alerts and anomaly detection
Concrete experience Built operations dashboards for marketplaces across multiple European markets. Implemented driver funnel analytics with conversion tracking from acquisition to activation.
